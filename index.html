🧮 S-Corp Stock Basis Ordering Rule

According to the IRS, in what order must adjustments be made to an S-Corporation shareholder's stock basis?

  1. 1st: Increase for Income Items (Taxable & Tax-Exempt)
  2. 2nd: Decrease for Distributions (Non-taxable)
  3. 3rd: Decrease for Non-deductible Expenses
  4. 4th: Decrease for Loss and Deduction Items

📜 Form 1041: Distributable Net Income (DNI)

DNI limits the amount on which a beneficiary can be taxed when receiving distributions from a Trust or Estate.

DNI Formula:
Taxable Income before Distribution Deduction
+ Personal Exemption
+ Capital Losses (allocated to principal)
- Capital Gains (allocated to principal)
+ Tax-Exempt Interest (minus expenses allocated to it)
